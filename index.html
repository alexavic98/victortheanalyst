About Me

Hi! My name is Victor and I am a data analyst with a strong background in SQL, Excel, and Power BI. To further my education and skills in data analytics, I decided to enroll in a program on Coursera offered by Google. I was thrilled to receive my Data Analytics Certificate, which has helped me to build a strong foundation in this field. In my current role as a data analyst, I use a variety of tools and techniques to analyze data and provide insights to my team and our clients. I also have a lot of experience using Excel, tableau and Power BI to visualize and present data in a clear and concise way. I am passionate about using data to drive change and am excited to continue learning and growing as a data analyst. In my free time, I enjoy staying active and hitting the gym. Overall, I am dedicated to using my skills and knowledge in data analytics to make a positive impact and help organizations make informed decisions.
